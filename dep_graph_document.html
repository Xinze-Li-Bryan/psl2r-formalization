Definition 9Almost minimizing

A surface \(\Sigma \) is \(\varepsilon \)-a.m. in \(U\) if there is no isotopy supported in \(U\) which keeps area within \(\varepsilon /8\) along the path and decreases the final area by at least \(\varepsilon \).

Lemma 10Convergence regularity

If a min–max sequence converges in the varifold sense, its limit contains a smooth embedded minimal surface.

Theorem 11Simon–Smith

Let \(M\) be a closed Riemannian \(3\)-manifold. For any saturated set \(\Lambda \) of generalized families of surfaces, there exists a min–max sequence obtained from \(\Lambda \) and converging in the varifold sense to a smooth embedded minimal surface with area \(m_0(\Lambda )\) (multiplicity allowed).
